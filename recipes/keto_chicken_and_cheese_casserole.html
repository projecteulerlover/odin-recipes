Keto Chicken and Cheese Casserole

Description

Keto-friendly take on chicken enchiladas without the tortillas.

This casserole is easily customized by adding any ingredients you have on hand.

I loved it as part of my first week of ketogenic recipes.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon butter, or as needed
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into chunks
  • 1 teaspoon taco seasoning mix, or to taste
  • 1 (8 ounce) jar salsa
  • Â½ cup sour cream
  • 4 ounces jalapeno peppers, diced
  • 4 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 4 green onions, sliced

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Butter a baking pan.
  2. Heat a pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Add chicken and saute until no longer pink in the center and juices run clear, 6 to 8 minutes.
  4. Drain excess liquid. Add taco seasoning; toss to coat. Transfer chicken into the prepared baking pan.
  5. Mix salsa, sour cream, and jalapenos together in a bowl and pour over the chicken.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until hot, about 25 minutes.
  7. Remove from oven and cover with Cheddar cheese.
  8. Continue baking until cheese melts, about 5 minutes more.
  9. Let casserole cool before cutting into pieces and garnishing with green onions.
